The basic rules of snooker

  • The red balls are worth 1 point once potted
  • The yellow ball is worth 2 points once potted
  • The green ball is worth 3 points once potted
  • The brown ball is worth 4 points once potted
  • The blue ball is worth 5 points once potted
  • The pink ball is worth 6 points once potted
  • The black ball is worth 7 points once potted
  • Once a red ball is potted the player can then pot a coloured ball of their choice and the cycle repeats
